leafy seadragon

the leafy sea dragon(phycodurus eques) is the only member of the genus phycodurus and is a marine fish in the family syngnathidae, which includes seadragon, pipefish,and seahorses.[2]

description

much like the seahorse, the leafy sea dragon's name is derived from its resembles to another creature (inthe case, the mythic drangon). while not large, they are slightly larger than most seahorses, growing to about 20-24 cm (8-9.5in). they feed on plankton and small crustaceans.
